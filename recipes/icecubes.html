Odin's Ice Cubes

Description

For those who don't know how or have forgotten how to make ice when the ice tray is empty.

Ingredients

  • A water source (clean one if possible)
  • Some tray, with well-sized square indents, to individually contain above water.

Steps

  1. Grab your ice cubes tray.
  2. Empty the ice cubes that are left in the tray, if there are any left, into the sink, leave alone to melt or run hot water on for a bit.
  3. Take the tray over to the sink and fill the holes, not the whole tray, with cold water. Hot water will freeze faster and be more clear.
  4. Open the freezer door.
  5. Place the water filled ice trays back in the freezer.
  6. Close the freezer door.
  7. Be sure to leave for around 4-6 hours at least to make sure it is frozen.
  8. It is a good idea to replace ice cubes that have sat for too long due to sublimation, if there are even any left.
